About

This is an application that prompts you to enter your birthday and depending on your gender outputs your Akan Name. Derived from the Ghanian culture, children are frequently given their first name corresponding to the day of the week they were born. Be the first to find out your Akan name now! All you need to do is fill in the form below with your birthday and click on the submit button and immediately get your Akan name!
